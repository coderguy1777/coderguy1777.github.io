The Project is still in development, and will have the beta version



Quick-time: The Revenge of the Mad Scientist

Quick-time: The Revenge of the Mad Scientist is a game built off of the Unity 3D Game Engine, coded in C#, with all Models and textures being made in either the Modeling Program Cinema 4D or Blender 2.79b, with Textures and Post Production work being done inside industry standard modeling software, such as substance designer, substance paint, and a plethora of adobe products as well.
